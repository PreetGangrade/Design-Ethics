About


Tech platforms make billions of dollars keeping us clicking, scrolling, and sharing. Just like a tree is worth more as lumber and a whale is worth more dead than alive—in the attention extraction economy a human is worth more when we are depressed, outraged, polarized, and addicted.

This attention extraction economy is accelerating the mass degradation of our collective capacity to solve global threats, from pandemics to inequality to climate change. If we can’t make sense of the world while making ever more consequential choices, a growing ledger of harms will destroy the futures of our children, democracy and truth itself.

Methods

Empowering

Empowering design ensures products center on the value they provide to people over the revenue it can generate.

Finite

Finite design maximizes the overall quality of time spent by bounding the experience and prioritizing meaningful and relevant content.

Inclusive

Inclusive design is a methodology that enables and draws on the full range of human diversity.

Resilient

Resilient design focuses on the well-being of the most vulnerable and anticipates the potential for abuse.

Respectful

Respectful design prioritizes people’s time, attention and overall digital well-being.

Thoughtful

Thoughtful design uses friction to prevent abuse, protect privacy, and steer people towards healthier digital habits.

Transparent

Transparent design is clear about intentions, honest in actions and free of dark patterns.
